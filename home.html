    Short Biography

    Xiaotian YU (余晓填 in Chinese) is currently a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science and Engineering in The Chinese University of Hong Kong . He expects to get the Ph.D. degree in 2019, and his research interests include sequential decisions making, machine learning, data mining and statistical learning. He has published several papers in top conferences, such as, AAAI, IJCAI, UAI and ICDM. He is a member of IEEE, ACM and AAAI.
